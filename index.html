A Legacy of Luxury

Born from the marriage of two rich fragrance traditions, Royal Oud Paris represents the pinnacle of French-Arabian perfumery. Created in the heart of Paris and infused with the finest Oud from the Arabian Peninsula, each bottle tells a story of uncompromising excellence.

Each creation of Royal Oud Paris demands extraordinary patience... 1000-year-old Oud wood, harvested from the heart of Assam forests. The finest French grape alcohol, distilled in Burgundy's ancient cellars. Rose essence from the Grasse Valley, perfumery's sacred origin. Precious amber oil extracted from the depths of Baltic shores. Two years of meticulous refinement, nothing less for perfection.
